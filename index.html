Welcome to our MedicoBot

Disease Detection is a very important use-case in the Healthcare Industry. There is no existing personalized conversational platform which detects diseases based on patient's symptoms and redirects to the nearest hospital based on the user's location. Apart from this Mental Health is also an alarming topic and we want people to talk about it with any hesitation. Critical Mental Health services are affected in 93% countries worldwide due to COVID19 acc. to WHO

We have designed a Chatbot, which will interact with the patients and diagnose the potential disease from which a person can be suffering and also the severity level of that disease (eg. Home Care, See a Doctor, or Ambulance). In the same chatbot one can take, Mental Health Test. The Mental Health Test is built with high standards. It implements the study of PHQ-SADS, PHQ-15 & GAD-7. It helps in diagnosis of Stress, Anxiety, Depression and also looks out for Somatic Symptom Disorder. Our Solution will also be providing medical assistance like recommending the nearest Hospital based on the current location detected by GPS. The chatbot will engage users in conversation.
